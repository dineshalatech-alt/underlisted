🗓️ Smart build order (don't hire all 11 at once)

Phase 1 — Finish the launch & protect quality (now): Forge (reliability), Vera (accuracy/QA), Penny (cost guard). These make sure what we ship works, the numbers are honest, and costs stay safe.
Phase 2 — Win & keep customers (at/after launch): Serena (growth), Quill (content/SEO), Hazel (support). Bring people in and make them stay.
Phase 3 — Scale safely (as you grow): Portia (legal), Knox (security). Atlas guards the basics today; these go deep once there's revenue + real customer data.
