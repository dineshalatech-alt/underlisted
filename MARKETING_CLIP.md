## 4. Filming notes (simple)

- **Screen-record the real app** on your phone (or a phone-sized browser window) so it's true 9:16 vertical — don't film a laptop screen with a camera.
- **iPhone:** Control Center → Screen Recording. **Android:** Quick Settings → Screen record. **Or** record the browser at a 430px-wide window.
- Open the app to the **feed first**, then record one smooth take: scroll → stop on the green "Great deal" card → tap → detail with "11% below value" + cash number.
- Move the thumb **slowly and deliberately**; pause ~1s on the score and on the cash number so they're readable.
- Add captions in **CapCut / InShot** (free). Big bold text, top third, high contrast.
- **No voiceover needed.** Optional: a trending upbeat sound at low volume.
- End on a 2–3s **static offer card** (logo + "$12.99/mo for your first year") so the offer lingers.
- Export **1080×1920, 9:16**. Keep it **under 15s**.
