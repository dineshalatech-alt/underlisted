# Underlisted — 90-Day Marketing Plan

> **Goal:** paying subscribers. **Style:** faceless / brand-led (AI voiceovers + app screen-recordings), not a personal brand. **Effort:** 10+ hrs/week. **Budget:** lean to start, scale on proof. Written 2026-06-13.

---

## 0. The honest gate (read first)

The app DB is empty (refresh worker not running) and the payment button isn't wired.
**Do not run big paid-subscriber ads until BOTH are true:**

1. Worker is live → app shows **real homes** in searched cities.
2. **Payment works** ($12.99/mo intro checkout completes end-to-end).

Until then we run the engine in **waitlist + content mode** (cheap, compounding). The day the app converts, we flip the switch and pour fuel. Nothing is wasted — the content library and audience built in weeks 1–4 are what make the paid spike cheap.

**Readiness checklist before paid scale:**
- [ ] Worker cron live, DB filled with real listings
- [ ] Payment checkout completes ($12.99/mo)
- [ ] Analytics installed (GA4 or Plausible — free)
- [ ] Meta Pixel + TikTok Pixel + Google tag on site & app entry
- [ ] One landing page with the offer + the urgency hook
- [ ] Email welcome/nurture sequence ready (Resend)

## 1. Positioning & the hook

**One-liner:** "Zillow shows you homes; we tell you which U.S. homes are actually *underpriced* — and why."

**The hook (lead with this everywhere):**
> "Lock in **$12.99/mo for a year** — the price is rising to **$44.99** soon."

Scarcity + price-anchor. This is the single strongest conversion lever you have. Put a countdown / "founding price" frame on the landing page and in launch emails.

**The 4 moat points (use as content angles):**
1. Plain-English Deal Score (0–100) — no spreadsheets.
2. Fire/flood **insurance-risk warning** (nobody else leads with this).
3. **True monthly cost** (not just list price).
4. **Real cash you'd actually need** to buy.

## 2. The core engine: "Underpriced Home of the Day"

One repeatable, faceless format that IS the brand. Everything else orbits it.

**Format (60–90 sec, 9:16):**
- Screen-record the app showing one real underpriced home.
- AI voiceover (ElevenLabs / CapCut) + bold captions.
- Script beat: *Hook → the number → why it's a deal (Deal Score) → the catch (insurance risk / true cost) → "find deals like this → link."*
- Brand: light green #1D9E75, black text, never blue. Logo bug in corner.

**Why this wins:** faceless, repeatable daily, demonstrates the product live, creates FOMO, and naturally ends in "get access." One recording → cut into TikTok + Reels + Shorts + Pinterest Idea Pin. 1 hour of work = 4+ posts.

**All videos are produced IN-HOUSE** (no paid outside creators). You own the full pipeline below; one person can run it.

**Production stack (cheap/free):**
| Job | Tool | Cost |
|---|---|---|
| Screen record app | OBS / built-in | Free |
| AI voiceover | ElevenLabs | ~$5–22/mo |
| Edit + captions | CapCut | Free |
| Thumbnails/graphics | Canva | Free |
| Schedule everywhere | Metricool / Buffer | Free tier |

## 4. The funnel (content → cash)

```
Short video / pin / post
        ↓  (link in bio / pinned / ad)
Free "Check A Deal" page  ← your 2_Check_A_Deal.py is the lead magnet
        ↓  (email capture)
Waitlist email  → nurture sequence (3–5 emails)
        ↓  (the $12.99 urgency hook)
$12.99/mo paid subscriber
        ↓
Retargeting ads close the undecided
```

**Lead magnet = the free deal check.** Don't gate it. "Paste any address, get a free Deal Score." That single free taste is what earns the email and the trust to upsell.

## 5. Budget & ramp (lean → scale on proof)

You said budget is flexible if it brings a crowd. The smart move is **earn the right to scale**: spend tiny until the app converts, then pour fuel on what works.

| Phase | Monthly spend | Where it goes |
|---|---|---|
| **Tools (always)** | $0–30 | ElevenLabs + Canva Pro maybe; rest free tiers |
| **Phase 1 test** | $300–500 | $10–15/day Meta test creatives + $50 Reddit. Goal: find winning hooks. |
| **Phase 2 scale** | $1,000–1,500 | Pour into winning Meta creatives, add Spark Ads on best TikToks, branded Google search |
| **Phase 3 pour** | Scale to ROAS | If CAC < target, there is no cap — keep scaling winners + retargeting |

**Unit economics (why you can spend):** intro $12.99/mo → $29.99 later. A subscriber who stays ~12 months is worth **$150+** (LTV). That means you can profitably pay **$30–50 to acquire** a paying subscriber. Target CAC **under $25** at start, scale aggressively while it holds.

**Rule:** never scale spend on a metric you can't see. Pixels + analytics first.

## 6. The "big crowd at once" launch spike

You care about bringing a crowd at once. That's a **coordinated launch moment**, fired only when the app is ready:

1. **Warm the waitlist** — 2 teaser emails ("we go live Tuesday, founding $12.99 price").
2. **Product Hunt launch** (free, big spike of early adopters).
3. **Reddit + FB group posts** the same morning (value framing, not spam).
4. **Creator seeding** — pay 3–5 micro real-estate TikTokers ($50–200 each) to post your deal video same week.
5. **Paid spike** — turn Meta + TikTok ads to launch budget for 72 hours.
6. **Urgency close** — "founding $12.99 price ends Sunday."

A synchronized day-1 spike + scarcity deadline = the crowd-at-once effect, and it seeds the algorithm so organic keeps rolling after.

## 7. 90-day calendar

### Phase 0 — Week 0 (readiness, no spend)
- Finish worker + payment (engineering). Install analytics + all pixels.
- Set up accounts: TikTok, IG, YouTube, Pinterest, Reddit, Metricool, Meta Ads Manager.
- Build landing page with the $12.99 hook + countdown. Write welcome email sequence.
- Record first **5 "Underpriced Home of the Day"** videos (bank a buffer).

### Phase 1 — Weeks 1–4 (build engine, grow waitlist, tiny tests)
- Post the deal video **daily** to TikTok + Reels + Shorts; 3 Pinterest pins/week.
- 1 SEO blog/city page per week (expand your static `site/` generator).
- Value posts in 2 Reddit + 2 FB groups per week (no links first, build karma).
- **$10–15/day Meta test** on 3–5 creative hooks → find winners. $50 Reddit test.
- Nurture every new email. Track: signups, cost per signup, best hooks.

### Phase 2 — Weeks 5–8 (turn on paid, drive subscribers)
- App is converting → switch ads to **subscriber** goal.
- Scale Meta to winning creatives ($30–50/day). Spark Ads on top TikToks.
- Launch **branded Google search** + retargeting.
- A/B test the landing page + the $12.99 hook copy.
- Start referral/affiliate ("give a friend a free month").

### Phase 3 — Weeks 9–12 (the crowd spike + scale)
- Run the **launch spike playbook** (§6) on a set date with the founding-price deadline.
- Scale spend on anything with CAC under target. Kill losers weekly.
- Heavy retargeting on free-deal-check users who didn't subscribe.
- Double down on the 2–3 channels that actually produced paid subs; drop the rest.
