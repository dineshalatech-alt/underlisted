Underlisted — 90-Day Marketing Plan

Goal: paying subscribers  ·  Style: faceless / brand-led (AI voice + app screen-recordings)  ·  Effort: 10+ hrs/week  ·  2026-06-13

0. The honest gate (read first)

Don't spend big on subscriber ads yet. Your app DB is empty (refresh worker not running) and the payment button isn't wired. Sending a paid crowd to a product that can't show real homes or take payment burns money.

Run in waitlist + content mode until BOTH are true: (1) worker live → app shows real homes; (2) payment works end-to-end ($12.99/mo). Then flip the switch and pour fuel. Nothing is wasted — the content & audience built in weeks 1–4 make the paid spike cheap.

Readiness checklist before paid scale:

  • ☐ Worker cron live, DB filled with real listings
  • ☐ Payment checkout completes ($12.99/mo)
  • ☐ Analytics installed (GA4 or Plausible — free)
  • ☐ Meta Pixel + TikTok Pixel + Google tag on site & app
  • ☐ Landing page with the offer + urgency hook
  • ☐ Email welcome/nurture sequence ready (Resend)

1. Positioning & the hook

One-liner: "Zillow shows you homes; we tell you which U.S. homes are actually underpriced — and why."
"Lock in $12.99/mo for a year — the price is rising to $44.99 soon."

Scarcity + price anchor. This is your strongest conversion lever — put a "founding price" countdown on the landing page and in every launch email.

4 moat angles for content:

  • 1 Plain-English Deal Score (0–100) — no spreadsheets.
  • 2 Fire/flood insurance-risk warning — nobody else leads with this.
  • 3 True monthly cost, not just list price.
  • 4 Real cash you'd actually need to buy.

2. Core engine: "Underpriced Home of the Day"

One repeatable, faceless format that is the brand. Everything orbits it.

The format (60–90s, vertical 9:16)

  • Screen-record the app showing one real underpriced home.
  • AI voiceover + bold captions.
  • Script beat: Hook → the number → why it's a deal (Deal Score) → the catch (insurance/true cost) → "find deals like this → link."
  • Brand: green #1D9E75, black text, never blue. Logo in corner.

Why it wins: faceless, daily, demonstrates the product live, creates FOMO, ends in "get access." One recording → TikTok + Reels + Shorts + Pinterest. 1 hour = 4+ posts.

All videos made in-house — no paid outside creators. You own the full pipeline; one person runs it.

4. The funnel (content → cash)

Short video / pin / post ↓ (link in bio / pinned / ad) Free "Check A Deal" page ← your lead magnet ↓ (email capture) Waitlist email → nurture (3–5 emails) ↓ (the $12.99 urgency hook) $12.99/mo paying subscriber ↓ Retargeting ads close the undecided
Lead magnet = the free deal check. Don't gate it. "Paste any address, get a free Deal Score." That free taste earns the email and the trust to upsell.

Budget is flexible — so earn the right to scale: spend tiny until the app converts, then pour fuel on what works.

Why you can spend: intro $12.99/mo → $29.99 later. A subscriber who stays ~12 months is worth $150+ (LTV). So you can profitably pay $30–50 to acquire a paying subscriber. Target CAC under $25 at start; scale hard while it holds. Never scale a metric you can't see — pixels + analytics first.

6. The "big crowd at once" launch spike

The crowd-at-once effect = a coordinated launch moment, fired only when the app is ready:

  1. Warm the waitlist — 2 teaser emails ("we go live Tuesday, founding $12.99 price").
  2. Product Hunt launch — free, big early-adopter spike.
  3. Reddit + FB groups same morning (value framing, not spam).
  4. Creator seeding — pay 3–5 micro real-estate TikTokers ($50–200 each) to post your deal video that week.
  5. Paid spike — Meta + TikTok ads at launch budget for 72 hours.
  6. Urgency close — "founding $12.99 price ends Sunday."

A synchronized day-1 spike + scarcity deadline brings the crowd and seeds the algorithm so organic keeps rolling after.

7. 90-day calendar

Phase 0 — Week 0 (no spend)

  • Finish worker + payment. Install analytics + all pixels.
  • Set up accounts: TikTok, IG, YouTube, Pinterest, Reddit, Metricool, Meta Ads.
  • Landing page with $12.99 hook + countdown. Write welcome emails.
  • Bank 5 "Underpriced Home of the Day" videos.

Phase 1 — Weeks 1–4 (engine + waitlist)

  • Deal video daily → TikTok + Reels + Shorts; 3 pins/week.
  • 1 SEO city page/week (expand site generator).
  • Value posts in 2 Reddit + 2 FB groups/week (no links first).
  • $10–15/day Meta test on 3–5 hooks + $50 Reddit. Nurture every email.

Phase 2 — Weeks 5–8 (turn on paid)

  • App converts → switch ads to subscriber goal.
  • Scale Meta winners ($30–50/day). Spark Ads on top TikToks.
  • Launch branded Google search + retargeting.
  • A/B the landing page + hook. Start referral ("free month for a friend").

Phase 3 — Weeks 9–12 (spike + scale)

  • Run the launch-spike playbook on a set date with the founding-price deadline.
  • Scale spend on anything under target CAC. Kill losers weekly.
  • Heavy retargeting on free-deal-check users who didn't subscribe.
  • Double down on the 2–3 channels that produced paid subs; drop the rest.
