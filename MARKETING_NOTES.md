## The one-liner (approved)

> **"Zillow shows you homes; DealCheck makes you do the math. We just tell you
> which California homes are actually good deals — and why."**

---

## Pricing copy (use everywhere)

- Standard: **"$12.99/mo for your first 12 months, then $29.99/mo — cancel anytime"**
- Hook: **"Lock in $12.99/mo for a year — goes up to $29.99 soon."**

---

## Video / ad hooks (first-2-seconds scroll-stoppers)

- "This California house is listed **10% below** what it's worth."
- "Here's how to spot an under-priced home in **10 seconds**."
- "Zillow won't tell you if it's a *good deal*. This does."
- "How much cash do you *actually* need to buy this house? Watch."

Each video: one idea, green score moment + "10% below value" + cash-needed, end on
the offer ("$12.99/mo for your first year").
