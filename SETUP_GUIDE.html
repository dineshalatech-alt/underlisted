🚀 Setup Guide — get online + turn on email

Two free accounts, about 10 minutes. No coding. Just follow the steps.

Part 1 · Put your deal pages online (free)

This makes your site folder a real website people can find on Google. Easiest way: Netlify Drop — you literally drag a folder onto a web page.

1
Open your site folder. It's here:
C:\Users\dines\OneDrive\Documents\14. Real Estate\site
(Tip: open File Explorer, paste that path in the address bar, press Enter.)
2
In your web browser, go to app.netlify.com/drop.
3
Drag the whole site folder onto that page and drop it. Wait ~30 seconds.
4
You'll get a live link like something-random.netlify.app. That's your site — open it!
5
Click Sign up (free) to keep the site, rename it, and later add your own web address (domain).
To update the site later: in the project, run python tools/gen_site.py, then drag the site folder onto Netlify again. (We can automate this later.)
Want your own name (like yourbrand.com)? Buy one (~$10/yr at Namecheap or Cloudflare) and connect it in Netlify under "Domain settings." Optional, do it anytime.

Part 2 · Turn on email alerts (free)

This lets the app email people "a deal just dropped!". We'll use Resend — a free, beginner-friendly email service (3,000 emails/month free).

1
Go to resend.com and click Sign up (free). Verify your email.
2
In the dashboard, open API Keys → Create API Key → Copy it. It starts with re_.
3
Open your .env file (in the project folder) and add this line, pasting your key after the =:
RESEND_API_KEY=re_your_key_here
Save the file.
4
Tell me "email done" — I'll wire the alert emails and send a test to you first.
Note: for testing, Resend lets you send from a built-in address. To email real customers from your brand later, you "verify your domain" in Resend (a 5-minute step we can do once you have a domain).
