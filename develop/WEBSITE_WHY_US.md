## WHY UNDERLISTED (5 simple benefit cards — clean, equal, scannable)

**1. 🏡 The biggest decision of your life**
You'll likely spend more on this than anything else you ever buy. We help you get it right —
before you commit, not after.

**2. ✅ Is it a good deal?**
One plain-English **Deal Score, 0–100**. Green means good. No spreadsheets, no jargon — just an
honest answer on the price.

**3. 💵 Can you actually afford it?**
See your **true monthly cost**, the **real cash** you'll need up front, and **how much you'd have
left over** each month. (This is the question no one else answers.)

**4. ⚠️ What could surprise you?**
We flag **fire & flood insurance risk** and other budget-busters *before* they cost you thousands.

**5. 👋 Built for you — not investors**
Other tools are made for flippers and full of jargon (cap rate, IRR, BRRRR). We just tell you,
simply, in words anyone can understand.
