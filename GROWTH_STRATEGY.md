# Growth Strategy — How we become the #1 U.S. deal-finding site

*Last updated: 2026-06-12.*

## The honest truth up front

Being "#1" is **two games**, and most people only think about the first:
1. **A product people love** (features) — gets people to **stay**.
2. **Distribution** (how people find us) — gets people **in the door**.

Zillow and Redfin won mostly on **#2** — search ranking (SEO), a free tier, and
brand — not because their tools were the best. So the biggest lever for us is **not
more features** — it's reach. We also can't out-Zillow Zillow head-on.

**We win by owning a WEDGE:** *"the simplest way for a normal person to find
genuinely good-value and foreclosure homes — and avoid the insurance traps that
quietly kill a deal."* Dominate that, then widen.

- **Realistic near-term #1:** the best site for *"find underpriced / foreclosure
  deals, explained simply."*
- **Literal #1 overall** (beating Zillow): a multi-year, funded journey. Aim the
  wedge first.

## Part 1 — What to ADD to the product (so people love it & refer others)

### A. Trust & accuracy (the non-negotiable foundation)
A #1 site is *trusted*. That means: **real photos** (fix Google), accurate value/rent,
the transparent "why it scored X," and honest labels (estimate vs. real). Get this
right before anything fancy.

### B. Lean HARD into the moat (our unfair advantage)
- **Insurance-COST estimator** — not just a fire/flood *flag*, but an estimated **$/mo
  insurance** (incl. flood/fire). This is the catch nobody else shows. Huge.
- **"True monthly cost"** = mortgage + property tax + insurance (incl. flood/fire) +
  HOA. The honest all-in number a beginner actually needs.

### C. The retention engine (what actually makes a site #1 = people come BACK)
- **Deal alerts + saved searches** (email/push/text): *"tell me when a good deal hits
  my area / a price drops."* THE habit-forming feature — the reason to return daily.
  (Premium tier.)
- **Watchlist / saved homes** + a **daily/weekly "best new deals" digest** email.
- **Free accounts** (so people can save and get alerts).

### D. Coverage & freshness
Full **nationwide listings** (eventually a proper MLS/RESO feed), **foreclosures**, and
**fast refresh**. Comprehensive + fresh = trust = repeat visits.

### E. Glance & discovery (the approved visual upgrades)
**Map + deal heatmap by ZIP**, letter grades / stars, side-by-side compare, price
history. (Already approved in COMPETITIVE_IDEAS / MASTER_SPEC.)

### F. Features that double as marketing (growth built into the product)
- **Free public city/ZIP deal pages** — *"Best deals in Austin, TX."* An SEO magnet
  (see Part 2).
- **Free address lookup** — *"Is this a good deal?"* — top of the funnel.
- **Shareable deal reports** — every share is free advertising (virality).

### G. For real-estate PROS (agents, investors, wholesalers)
A **pro tier**: exports, **client-shareable branded reports**, **farm-area deal alerts**,
and an **API**. Pros bring volume and credibility.

### H. Later
**Mobile app** (every #1 consumer site has one) and an **AI assistant**
(natural-language search + plain-English explanations — the 2026 trend).

## Part 2 — How to REACH everyone (this is the real game)

### 1. SEO — the #1 long-term engine (how Zillow/Redfin actually won)
**Programmatically generate a page for every city, ZIP, and query** — "foreclosures in
<city>," "cheapest homes in <ZIP>," "underpriced homes in <county>." That's **millions
of search-entry pages** that compound for years. This is the single biggest lever.

### 2. Short-form video — reach "normal people" fast
TikTok / Instagram Reels / YouTube Shorts showing **real deals** (we already have the
clip plan in MARKETING_CLIP). Cheap, repeatable, can go viral. Post consistently,
double down on what hits.

### 3. Freemium — free tier = reach + word of mouth
Let **anyone search and see a deal score for free**; charge for **alerts, foreclosures,
and pro tools**. A free tier is the top of the funnel AND fuels SEO and sharing. (Strong
recommendation: do this.)

### 4. Data-story PR (high leverage — we already have the data)
Publish reports: *"The 50 most underpriced ZIP codes in America," "Best foreclosure
deals of 2026."* Press picks these up → **backlinks → SEO authority**. This is exactly
how Reventure grew. Our FEMA/FHFA/foreclosure data makes these stories free to produce.

### 5. Communities where deal-hunters already are
Reddit (r/realestateinvesting, r/FirstTimeHomeBuyer), **BiggerPockets**, Facebook groups,
Discord. Be genuinely helpful (share real finds), not spammy.

### 6. Reach the real-estate PROS
Partner with **agents, investors, and wholesalers**; an **affiliate/referral program**;
show up at REI meetups, BiggerPockets, and agent Facebook groups. The **pro tier +
lead-gen** brings them and their deal flow.

### 7. Email — the audience you own
Capture emails via the free tools, send the **"best new deals" digest.** Owned audience
you don't pay for twice.

### 8. Creators / influencers
Partner with real-estate & first-time-buyer **YouTubers/TikTokers** — they have the exact
audience.

### 9. Referral program
*"Share a deal / refer a friend."* Built-in virality.

### 10. Paid ads — LATER
Google/Meta retargeting **only once the funnel converts.** Don't pay to fill a leaky
bucket; earn the free/organic channels first.
