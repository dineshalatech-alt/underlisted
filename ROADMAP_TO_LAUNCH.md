## Phase 6 — Marketing video plan (record only; do NOT make now)

**Goal:** short, vertical demo videos that show the app finding a *real* under-priced
U.S. deal **fast**, to drive trial sign-ups.

**Format & platforms**
- Vertical (9:16) for **TikTok, Instagram Reels, YouTube Shorts**.
- **15–30 seconds** each.

**What each video shows**
- Scroll the feed → land on an under-priced home.
- The **green deal score** pops.
- The plain-English signal: **"10% below value."**
- The **cash-needed breakdown** (the "this is what you'd actually need" moment).

**Rules for a video that converts**
- **Hook in the first 2 seconds** (the scroll-stopper).
- **One clear idea per video** — don't cram features.
- **End with the offer:** "$12.99/mo for your first year."

**Production approach**
- Make **3–5 variations** (different hooks / different homes / different angle).
- **Post consistently**, then **double down on whatever gets views.**

**Dependency (record AFTER these are true):** the app must be **visually polished**,
the **Deal Score must work**, and **photos must be fixed** — a demo has to look
real. Do not record until all three hold.
