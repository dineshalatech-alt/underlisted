=====================================================================
THE PRODUCT
=====================================================================
A real-estate DEAL-FINDER, CALIFORNIA ONLY for now (built so adding more states
later = new config + risk source, not a rewrite). Goal: so simple that a Gen Z
first-timer AND a 75-year-old can open it, scroll a feed, and instantly spot a good
deal. Mobile-first. It's intended to become a paid product. Keep data, scoring,
financing, and UI as separate modules.

CURRENT STATE: scaffold, listings, cost architecture, background worker, cloud-DB
switch, location/photos, and rent/value estimates are CODED but UNTESTED against
live APIs. Deal Score, cash-needed module, risk flags, map, AI renders, branding,
accounts, and payments are NOT built.

DATA SOURCES (keys in .env; never print keys; do NOT scrape Redfin/Zillow):
- RentCast (RENTCAST_API_KEY): sale listings, value estimate (AVM), rent estimate.
- Google Street View Static (STREETVIEW_API_KEY): real curb photo.
- Google Maps Static (same Google key, "Maps Static API" enabled): satellite/aerial.
- Google Maps link: free clickable address link (no API).
- Google Gemini / Nano Banana (GEMINI_API_KEY): AI "imagine remodeled" renders +
  one-time branding assets ONLY. NEVER use AI to depict the real current property.
- Cal Fire (CA fire zones) + FEMA (flood): free public risk data.

COST ARCHITECTURE (applies to everything):
- Shared cache keyed by PROPERTY, not per user (500 users viewing one home = one
  lookup + one image fetch total).
- TTLs (listings ~24h, value/rent ~7d, images ~never), all config.
- No fetch on page load — users read cache; refreshes come from the scheduled
  worker. Incremental sync only. Lazy-load images (on open). Per-user monthly cap.
- Smallest-good image size. Expensive calls behind explicit buttons.
- Admin/Usage view: billable calls by source, image fetches, cache hit-rate, est. $.

VISUAL / BRANDING (clean-green theme): primary #1D9E75, light fill #E1F5EE, deep
#0F6E56, neutral grays; reserve amber/red for caution/bad signals. Big readable
text, big tap targets, plain language, one clear action per screen, jargon gets a
one-tap "what's this?" tooltip. Show "why it's a deal" visually (score gauge,
simple bars) so it's understood WITHOUT reading.

DISCLAIMERS in-app: values/rents/cash are ESTIMATES, not financial advice; verify
with a licensed lender/agent. Fair Housing: scoring/filtering must NOT use signals
that proxy for protected classes (no demographic or "neighborhood quality" inputs).

PHASE 1 — FINISH THE CORE PRODUCT (California)
=====================================================================
- DEAL SCORE (0-100), weights in config: mainly how far BELOW estimated value it's
  listed, plus rent yield, with days-on-market and risk as secondary. Flag "good
  deals" above a threshold. Show a "why it scored X" breakdown (segmented bar) and
  the comps + a value RANGE so the score is trustworthy.
- BACKTEST the score against recently-sold homes; report whether it would have
  flagged the genuinely good ones.
- "HOW MUCH CASH YOU REALLY NEED" (beginner-first): lead with ONE big number ("you
  need about $X available"). Break into money that LEAVES your account (down
  payment + closing = cash to close) vs. money you KEEP (reserves). Toggle "live in
  it" (3-3.5% down) vs "rent it out" (20-25% down) recomputes everything. Credit
  band + typical qualifying score. Rate is a config value. Tooltips on every term.
  Advanced collapsible section (PITI, cap rate, yield, DTI). Optional affordability
  filter. All labeled estimates.
- RISK FLAGS: FEMA flood + Cal Fire fire-zone badges, plus a "check insurance cost"
  note for fire zones. Optional/bonus — app must not depend on them.
- SIMPLE FEED UX: mobile-first scrolling feed of listings; each card = photo, price,
  address, beds/baths/sqft, and a clear good-deal signal (green score / below-market
  badge). Tap → plain-language detail (Street View + aerial toggle, Google Maps
  link, rent + value-vs-price, risk, tooltips). No setup before results appear.
- MAP VIEW: score-colored pins (optional).
EXIT TEST: I show it to 3-5 real people; they find a deal without help.
