2. The ladder — what 100k impressions is really worth

Impressions are the top of a funnel. Mid-range industry benchmarks turn them into customers like this:

100,000 impressions (people see us) ↓ × ~1.5% click (range 1–3%) 1,500 visit Underlisted ↓ × ~8% give email (strong free deal-check; range 3–15%) 120 emails / waitlist ↓ × ~5% become paid (range 2–10%) ~6 paying subscribers → ~$78/mo recurring (at $12.99)
The executive truth: 100k impressions ≈ ~6 paying customers. That's a starting unit, not the destination. To build real revenue you need millions of impressions — so the whole strategy is about making each impression as cheap as possible by stacking free organic + borrowed audiences (agents/creators) on top of paid.
